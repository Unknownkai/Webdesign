Biography

Born in Japan but raised in America from a young age, my background is a blend of cultural experiences. While I dedicated much of my youth to football, my passion evolved as I transitioned to KSU. In my senior year of high school, I discovered a love for throwing the discus, a sport I haven't actively pursued but deeply desire to explore further.

Currently, my focus lies within the community of the water polo club at KSU, where I find joy alongside my commitment to physical fitness. Balancing my time between the demanding schedule of a water polo, gym, and weekend forklift operator at a book distribution center leaves little time for myself, but I enjoy it.

My goals are not limited to what I'm doing right now. I aiming for academic excellence and straight As, I'm also committed to learning Japanese so that I can re-establish a connection with my family. I also have a strong commitment to developing my own strengths, both mentally and physically. I approach every day with enthusiasm and dedication, finding fulfillment in the variety of my experiences, even in spite of the challenges presented by my weekend job.
